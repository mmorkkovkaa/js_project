А little about the clans of the hidden village

Uchiha clan

one of the four noble clans of the Hidden Leaf, considered the village's strongest clan due to their Sharingan as well as innate martial prowess.After helping to found Konoha decades ago, the Uchiha became increasingly isolated from the affairs of the village, leading to even more deaths during the Uchiha Clan Massacre. Some Uchiha still live today.

more

Uzumaki clan

was a prominent clan from Hidden Maelstrom Village. Since its disbandment, many of its known members have resided in Konoha, including the Fifth and Seventh Hokage as direct descendants.

more

Senju clan

was one of the clans responsible for establishing the first shinobi village, the Hidden Leaf Village, while the second was their former rival, the Uchiha clan. Although the clan itself as a group apparently did not last long, the Senju continue to influence the politics of Konoha through their philosophy of the Will of Fire, as well as the corresponding interpretation of the role of the Hokage title.

more

Nara Clan

is a clan known for their high level of intelligence, guarding deer like Rikumaru, as well as their ability to control shadows with the help of the Element Yin. Shikamaru Nara is the last current head of the clan.
